About MediCheck

MEDICHECK is a digital platform dedicated to the verification of pharmaceutical drugs to combat the growing threat of counterfeit medications in our society. By allowing users to check the authenticity of drugs using batch numbers and NAFDAC verification, MEDICHECK empowers patients, pharmacists, and healthcare professionals to make safer health decisions. In addition to verification tools, the platform also provides drug inventory features to track and manage legitimate medications for personal or institutional use.


Our Mission

Our mission at MEDICHECK is to protect public health by promoting access to authentic and safe medications. We aim to bridge the gap between regulatory drug data and everyday users by offering a simple, reliable platform for drug verification. Through technology and collaboration, we strive to reduce the circulation of counterfeit drugs and enhance trust in medical prescriptions across Nigeria and beyond.


Our Values

At the heart of MEDICHECK are the values of integrity, transparency, and public safety. We are committed to delivering accurate drug information, maintaining user privacy, and supporting regulatory agencies like NAFDAC in their mission to ensure pharmaceutical quality. We believe that everyone from the rural patient to the urban pharmacist deserves tools that help them verify medications and safeguard health.
